A chatbot is an artificial intelligence-powered program designed to simulate human conversation through text or speech interactions. It can understand user input, process it using natural language processing algorithms, and respond with relevant information or actions. Chatbots are used across various industries to automate customer support, provide information, and execute tasks. They are versatile tools that can enhance user experiences, streamline processes, and offer personalized assistance on websites, messaging apps, and other digital platforms. By continuously learning from interactions, chatbots become more intelligent, efficient, and capable of handling complex conversations.
